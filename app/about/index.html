MonkeyBars was born out of the need for a place to explore and tinker.

Our Values

If we were a tasty, homemade cake these would be our ingredients. Everybody likes cake.

Learn
Failure created penicillin, potato chips and other wonders. Naturally, MonkeyBars believes in the process of falling and getting back up with every space we create.

Collaborate
Nothing great was ever built alone. Ever. MonkeyBars believes in the power of diverse, supportive teamwork when embarking on any worthwhile journey.

Build
Ideation is a great exercise but, at some point, you have to just do it. MonkeyBars provides the people, the place, the tools and the grub. What are you waiting for?
